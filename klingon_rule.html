The Current Rule

Over the past 60 years, we have seen ebbs and flows to our unemployment rate. Within the last few years the unemployment rate as skyrocketed and as a major factor for a our growing economy, we need to understand what may be causing this blight and how to stop it. Romulans have predominantly controlled the government for the last 60 years, has their political campaigns and agendas fueled the socio-economic unrest?

Initial Findings

Using employment data sources provided by the ambassador from Earth, Jeff Parker, I have uncovered interesting insights into each political parties control and the employment repercussions. First, I viewed the overall unemployment rate over the last 60 years of data and which party was predominantly in control; in control is defined as having 2 branches of government with majority in the party.

The first graph shows that the Klingon party has had very little voice in the government while Romulans have controlled this world. Romulan control have brought a trend of increased unemployment rate over the years, while Klingon’s have attempted lower it while in control only to lead to a new spike during Romulan control.

# Calculate Overall Average

Deeper Dive

There are many ups and downs of unemployment during each parties rule, however, I dove deeper into the most recent years to figure out what how each party is influencing the deltas of employment and unemployment. Klingon control has shown positive delta’s in employment rate — higher number of citizens employment. Complementing their negative delta’s in unemployment — lower number of citizens unemployment.
